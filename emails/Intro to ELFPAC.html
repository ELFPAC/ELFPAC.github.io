An Update from ELFPAC on...

Cary Kennedy

Gubernatorial Candidate for Colorado

Quick Facts

  • Party: Democrat
  • Former Colorado State Treasurer
  • Former Deputy Mayor of Denver and the City's CFO
  • Colorado Native and Manual High School Graduate
  • Website: carykennedyforgovernor.com

Kennedy's Platform

  • Healthcare
    • Universal Healthcare via Health First Colorado (CO Medicaid) or a private plan
    • Wants increased access to contraception for women
  • Education
    • Increased funding for public education
    • Emphasis on college preparation in all schools
    • Increased vocational and concurrent enrollment programs
  • Energy and Environment
    • Implement emissions reduction standards which "exceed"
      ones set by the Paris Climate Accord
    • Increase regulations on oil and gas in the interest of public health and safety
    • Give local communities more autonomy over local fuel resources
    • Protection of public lands
    • Programs including "water-sharing" in order to conserve water,
      protect river ecosystems and agriculture
    • Fund research on agriculture in order to make it more sustainable
  • Social Justice
    • Aims to make a more "equitable" Colorado and advance
      Coloradans of color through "writing and implementing policies"
      that allow all people to succeed.
    • Advocates for a clean DACA
    • Anti-death penalty
    • Ban conversion therapy and protect LGBTQ+ from discrimination
      of businesses
    • Allow transgender people to have their gender of choice on state IDs and legal documents
  • Jobs and Economy
    • TABOR reform to decrease cuts to public education and stimulate economic growth
    • Advocate for equal pay for women
    • Increase wages for low- and middle-class workers, as well
      as reforming the tax system to decrease income inequality
  • Gun Violence
    • Common-sense gun control - universal background checks
    • Strict regulation of military grade weapons
